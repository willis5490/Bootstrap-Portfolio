I was born in North Kingstown, RI in 1990. I grew up in a nice quite neighborhood, a minute walk from the ocean. My first career was as a musician-which I loved dearly. Unfortunately there is not much money in music, so I had to find other sources of income. I decided to go to school for Massage Therapy at Bancroft, which is located in Worcester MA. That career allowed me to survive while still playing music at night. I eventually burned out and starting doing electrical work.

I did this for three years and learned quite a bit, but at the end of the day it was very repetitive. I went back to school for mechanical engineering and one day my friend convinced me to take a coding class. I was hooked! I find myself thinking about coding most hours of the day and I am always excited to figure out the next problem!
